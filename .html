Personal Project

FPGA Beamforming Array with Vision-Based Spatial Tracking

Building a beamforming microphone array that combines camera-based face tracking with FPGA signal processing to locate and follow a sound source in real time. The FPGA computes per-channel delay and phase adjustments across the array, coherently summing inputs into a spatially focused audio output.
